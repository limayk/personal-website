Kalyani Limaye

Hi, I'm Kalyani! I'm a master's student studying computational math at Stanford. I did my undergrad at UT Austin where I studied applied math, economics, and CS. I'm broadly interested in the behavioral aspect of economics and decision-making under uncertainty, and how those ideas carry over to agents and AI systems. I've done a graduate research assistantship in the Biomedical Data Science department at Stanford, working on clinical reinforcement learning. I previously worked as a quant at Goldman Sachs, modeling capital and counterparty stress exposure.
